What is Fundamental Value?
The Fundamental Value (FV) represents the intrinsic worth of a subnet token based on the operational costs it would take to replace the subnet's services.
How it's calculated:
FV = (Annual Operating Expenses √∑ 365) √∑ Daily Token Emissions
